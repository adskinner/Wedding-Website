Accommodations

If you are coming from out of town, we look forward to hosting you in our beloved home of Athens, Ga. Below is a list of reccommended accomodation options. Please contact us if you need any help getting something booked!

  • Airbnb We reccommend booking in the Normaltown & Boulevard neighborhoods.
  • Hotel Indigo A modern hotel located on the edge of bustling downtown. We love to visit their lounge for happy hour drinks.
  • Graduate Hotel An eclectic hotel that has personality in every detail. We love their coffee shop!
  • AthBNB A recently renovated bed & breakfast that has jaw-dropping style. We want to book a staycation here!

Athens Reccomendations

Welcome to Athens! Jeff and I have called Athens home for a very, very long time. Here's what we think makes Athens one-of-a-kind.

  • Athentic Brewing: This local craft brewery is near and dear to our hearts. Allison's parents co-founded Athentic Brewing in 2020 and it's been a wild ride ever since! Our Welcome Party on Friday Nov 17th will be at Athentic, but feel free to stop into the brewery on Saturday or Sunday to visit the family business again.
  • Puma Yu's: Thai-influenced restaurant and bar that was opened by our dear friend's Ally & Pete in 2022. The food and drink menu is stunning every time. We will be hosting our after party at Puma Yu's, but stop in for happy hour on Friday to experience their full offerings!
  • Botanical Gardens: Park that features beautiful, native gardens and trails.
  • Downtown & UGA North Campus: take a stroll through our classic downtown and then visit the neighborhing historic north campus of UGA.
